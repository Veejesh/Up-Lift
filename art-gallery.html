Famous Art Gallery

Mona Lisa

Painted by Leonardo da Vinci, the Mona Lisa is famous for her enigmatic expression and is considered a masterpiece of the Renaissance.

The Starry Night

By Vincent van Gogh, this artwork expresses his emotional turmoil and fascination with the night sky during his stay in an asylum.

Persistence of Memory

Salvador Dalí’s surreal masterpiece known for melting clocks, representing the distortion of time and dream-like states.
